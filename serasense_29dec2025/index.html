How it works?

Facing new challenges every day

Real-Time Strain Intelligence

StrainX continuously tracks physiological signals to measure stress, recovery, and body load in real time, transforming raw biomarker data into clear, actionable insights.

Multi-Sensor Biomarker Tracking

StrainX combines heart rate variability, electrodermal activity, skin temperature, sleep patterns, and sweat biomarkers to deliver a complete picture of physical and mental strain.

Actionable Stress Insights

StrainX translates complex physiological data into an easy-to-understand Strain Score, helping users identify stress triggers, optimize recovery, and prevent burnout.

Performance & Wellness

Designed for professionals, athletes, and wellness-focused users, StrainX supports smarter decisions around workload, rest, hydration, and daily performance.

Features

Our core features

REAL-TIME STRAIN INTELLIGENCE

StrainX continuously analyzes physiological signals to calculate a real-time Strain Score, helping users understand their current stress level and bodily load through live, data-driven insights.

  • Combines HR, HRV, EDA, & skin temperature.
  • Monitor strain levels throughout the day.
  • Detects acute stress spikes and overload.
  • Converts complex data into actionable scores.

MULTI-BIOMARKER MONITORING

StrainX integrates multiple biological and physiological inputs to deliver a complete picture of physical and mental strain, going beyond single-sensor wearables.

  • Tracks nervous, cardiovascular & thermal signals.
  • Supports sweat & hydration biomarker inputs.
  • Correlates activity, sleep, and recovery data.
  • Improves accuracy through sensor fusion.

SLEEP & RECOVERY INSIGHTS

StrainX evaluates sleep quality and recovery patterns to show how well the body resets from daily stress, enabling smarter decisions around rest and performance.

  • Measures sleep duration and efficiency.
  • Detects sleep disruption and awakenings.
  • Links poor sleep to next-day strain impact.
  • Provides recovery-focused insights and trends.
